K-Score System (0–100)

A composite score built from 5 weighted sub-scores. Graded A+/A/B/C/D/F. Refreshed 5× per day.

ComponentWeightWhat it measuresIdeal range
Technical35%Price action quality: RSI, MACD, trend alignment, SMA crossovers, ADX, momentum indicators≥65 = strong setup
Momentum25%Recent price velocity and volume: rate of price change, volume trend, relative strength≥60 = accelerating
Value20%Valuation attractiveness vs sector peers: P/E, P/B, earnings yield percentile ranking≥55 = fairly valued
Growth10%Revenue and earnings growth trajectory vs sector peers≥60 = growing faster than peers
Volatility10%Price stability (higher score = more stable, lower risk). High volatility stocks score lower.≥50 = acceptable risk
Fair Price—K-Score derived intrinsic value estimate. Shows % upside or overvaluation vs current price.Positive % = undervalued
Relative Strength (RS)—How this stock is performing vs other stocks in its sector. Percentile rank 0–100.≥60 = sector leader. Below 40 = lagging.

End-to-End Trading Workflow

A complete process from market morning to executing and managing trades. Follow this daily routine to get the most out of every feature.

1
Morning Check — Market Context
5–10 minutes · Do this before looking at any individual stock
①
Home Page → Market Overview
Read the macro environment
Check SPY/QQQ/HSI % change, VIX level, and Fear & Greed index. These set the risk backdrop for the whole day.
  • VIX > 25 → reduce position sizes, tighten stops
  • F&G < 30 (Extreme Fear) → suppression filters active, wait for stabilization
  • F&G > 75 (Extreme Greed) → be selective, avoid chasing
  • Market Breadth > 60% → healthy backdrop, act on setups
②
Home Page → Signal Changes Feed (bottom)
Check what flipped overnight
The Signal Change Feed shows every ▲ Upgrade (→ BUY) and ▼ Downgrade (→ SELL) across your watchlist in the last 48h. Start here to find today's candidates.
  • ▲ Upgrade with ≥36% confidence → add to today's watch list
  • ▼ Downgrade on a position you hold → review for exit
  • Multiple upgrades in same sector → sector momentum building
③
Rankings → Sector Rotation
Identify leading sectors
Check which sectors are leading (green) vs lagging (red) vs distributing. Trade with sector tailwinds, not against them.
  • Buy stocks in leading sectors (≥SPY+3%)
  • Avoid or short stocks in distributing sectors (≤SPY-5%)
2
Candidate Discovery — Find Setups
10–20 minutes · Multiple paths to find trade candidates
④
Opportunities Page
Use pre-built strategy tabs
Six curated strategy views do the filtering for you. Pick the tab that matches your trading style.
  • Swing tab → 5–30d trades with strong technical + BUY signal
  • Confluence tab → highest-conviction setups where signal + K-Score + technical all agree (≥65)
  • Short-Term tab → 1–5d momentum plays, often with volume spike
  • AI Signal tab → ranked by ML confidence
⑤
Screener → Preset Filters or Custom
Screen for specific conditions
Use one-click presets or build your own filter combination. Then review results.
  • Vol Surge ⚡ preset → volume ≥2× avg (unusual interest / potential breakout)
  • Strong BUY preset → BUY signal + K-Score ≥65 + Confidence ≥60%
  • Deep Value preset → ≥15% below fair price + P/E ≤20
  • Custom: add Min RS Score 60 to ensure you're only in sector leaders
  • Add Min Momentum 60 to ensure price is actually moving
⑥
Screener → AI Natural Language
Use plain English to screen
Type what you want in plain English. Examples:
  • "tech stocks with BUY signal and high momentum above 70"
  • "undervalued healthcare stocks with analyst buy rating"
  • "HK stocks with BUY signal and low PE below 15"
3
Deep Analysis — Validate the Setup
5–15 minutes per stock · Go to Stock Detail for each candidate
⑦
Stock Detail → AI Signal Tab (your horizon)
Check the Conviction Gate
A BUY signal means nothing without Gate confirmation. This is your first filter.
  • FULL → all 8 technical conditions pass. Strongest entry.
  • NEAR → 6–7 conditions pass. Acceptable if other factors align.
  • FAILED → don't enter. The technical foundation is broken.
  • Check which specific layers are failing — e.g., if only "RSI Bounds" fails, it may be temporarily overbought and recoverable.
⑧
Stock Detail → K-Score Panel
Validate stock quality
Read the 5 sub-scores. A strong setup has high Technical + Momentum, with acceptable Value and Growth.
  • K-Score ≥65 + Grade A or B = quality stock in good shape
  • Low Value score (below 40) is fine for growth stocks — they trade at a premium
  • Low Momentum (<40) despite BUY signal = momentum hasn't started yet. Higher risk.
  • Check Relative Strength: ≥60 = sector leader (best for longs)
  • Check Fair Price: positive % = stock is undervalued
⑨
Stock Detail → Research Panel
Check fundamental alignment
The AI Signal is technical. Research is fundamental. When they agree, conviction is highest.
  • STRONGLY ALIGNED (Signal BUY + Research STRONG BUY/BUY) → maximum conviction
  • DIVERGENT (Signal BUY + Research AVOID/SELL) → proceed very cautiously or skip
  • Check Conviction Score: ≥70 = high conviction. ≥80 = exceptional.
  • If research is stale (>14 days old), click "Generate Research" to refresh
⑩
Stock Detail → Technical Indicators + Volume Chart
Confirm technical picture
Verify the indicators independently of the AI signal.
  • RSI between 40–65 = sweet spot for BUY entries (not overbought)
  • MACD histogram positive AND rising = momentum accelerating
  • Weekly Alignment: CONFIRMED = both timeframes agree
  • Volume spike on an up day = institutional buying. Best entry signal.
  • Active chart pattern (double bottom, flag, cup) = technical setup confirms
⑪
Stock Detail → Signal Filter (or Signal Filter page)
Check suppression conditions
Even a BUY with Gate FULL can be suppressed. Check how many dots are active.
  • 0 dots = clean setup. Act with full size.
  • 1–2 yellow dots = minor suppression. Reduce size slightly.
  • 3+ dots or any red/orange dot = wait for conditions to clear
  • Weekly Gate (red) = the most important single suppressor. Never trade against a weekly downtrend.
⑫
Stock Detail → Earnings Calendar + Risk Metrics
Check risk factors before entry
Know your risks before entering.
  • Earnings within 7 days → high binary risk. Consider reducing size or waiting.
  • High Max Drawdown (≥35%) → stock has crashed before. Set tighter stops.
  • Short Trend ↑ (rising shorts) → bearish institutional pressure. Not disqualifying but a warning.
  • Low Sharpe (<0.5) → poor risk-adjusted returns historically
4
Trade Planning — Define Entry, Stop, Target
5–10 minutes · Use the Game Plan as your foundation
⑬
Stock Detail → Game Plan Panel
Generate and review the AI Game Plan
Click "Generate Game Plan" for an AI-drafted trade structure. Review every component critically — it's a starting point, not a guaranteed plan.
  • Entry Orders: limit prices near support or key levels. Use the lowest entry if you want the best risk:reward.
  • Stop Loss: this IS your risk. Know it before you enter. Loss = (Entry - Stop) × Shares.
  • Take Profit: initial target. Consider scaling out (sell half at TP1, trail the rest).
  • Key Risk: read this carefully. If the risk is "earnings surprise" and earnings are in 5 days, wait.
⑭
Stock Detail → ML Panel (Kelly Criterion)
Determine position size
Use Kelly Criterion as a guide for position sizing. Half-Kelly is conservative and recommended.
  • Confluence Score A (≥85): max 3% of portfolio
  • Confluence Score B (70–85): max 2% of portfolio
  • Confluence Score C (55–70): max 1% of portfolio
  • Never risk more than 1% of total capital on a single trade's stop loss
⑮
Stock Detail → AI Chat
Ask the AI specific questions
The AI chat has full context (price, signal, K-Score, analyst target, recent news). Use it to pressure-test your thesis.
  • "What are the main risks to a SWING BUY on this stock right now?"
  • "How does this stock's valuation compare to its sector peers?"
  • "What would need to happen for this BUY signal to fail?"
5
Set Alerts — Never Miss a Move
2–5 minutes per stock · Set before the trade, not after
⑯
Stock Detail → Price Alert Section (or Opportunities → Alert Suggestions)
Set entry trigger alerts
If you want to buy at a specific limit price, set a "price crosses above X" alert to notify you when it's close. The Opportunities page generates smart alert suggestions based on current technicals.
  • Support level break → alert if price drops below support (stop-loss trigger)
  • Resistance break → alert if price breaks above resistance (breakout entry)
  • RSI Oversold → alert when RSI drops below 35 (mean reversion setup)
  • Golden Cross → alert when SMA50 crosses above SMA200 (major bullish signal)
  • Recurring alerts → re-arm automatically (useful for ongoing monitoring)
⑰
Alerts Page
Manage all active alerts
View, edit, and delete all your price alerts in one place. See which have triggered and when.
  • Set take-profit alert when you enter a trade (price reaches your target)
  • Set stop-loss alert at your stop level
  • These are email alerts — check your email for notifications
6
Paper Trading — Test Before You Risk Real Money
Ongoing · Track every trade in the paper portfolio
⑱
Paper Portfolio Page
Run the strategy in simulation first
The paper portfolio auto-trades based on signals — or you can manually simulate entries. Review performance weekly.
  • Equity Curve: is your portfolio line beating SPY/QQQ? If not, why?
  • Win Rate: >55% is good for swing trading. Quality matters more than quantity.
  • Sharpe Ratio: ≥1.0 means you're getting good return for the risk you're taking
  • Monte Carlo P(Positive): probability your strategy is profitable by year end. Below 50% = rethink the strategy.
  • Use the benchmark comparison to see if you're actually adding alpha vs just riding the market
7
Ongoing Monitoring — Manage Open Positions
Daily · 5 minutes per day
⑲
Home Page → Watchlist + Signal Changes
Check for signal changes on open positions
Scroll to the Signal Change Feed every morning. A ▼ Downgrade on a position you hold is an exit signal.
  • BUY → HOLD: the setup is weakening. Tighten stops or take partial profits.
  • BUY → SELL: exit or reduce the position.
  • HOLD → BUY upgrade: add to the position if still within your risk limit.
⑳
Stock Detail → Volume Chart + Signal Filter
Watch for deterioration signals
For any open position, check these warning signs:
  • Weekly Gate turns red: immediate caution — weekly downtrend
  • Volume spike on a DOWN day: distribution (selling into weakness)
  • RSI > 70 + Stoch RSI > 80: overbought, take partial profits
  • MACD histogram turns negative: momentum has reversed
  • Earnings in <7 days: decide: hold through earnings (higher risk) or exit before
8
Review & Improve — Weekly Analysis
Weekly · 20–30 minutes
㉑
Signal Accuracy Page
Review signal performance
See the historical win rate, average return, and Sharpe ratio of signals you've followed. Are you executing on the best signals?
㉒
Trade Performance Page
Analyze closed trades
Review every closed paper trade: duration, exit reason, P&L. Look for patterns in what's working and what's not.
  • Are your losses bigger than your wins? Fix your stop loss discipline.
  • Are most wins from Confluence A setups? Focus there.
  • Is a particular horizon (SHORT/SWING) underperforming? Adjust your focus.
㉓
Journal Page
Log observations and lessons
Write down what worked and what didn't each week. The pattern recognition builds over time and is the most valuable thing you can do to improve.
